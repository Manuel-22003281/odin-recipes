THE BEST 3-CHEESE WHITE PIZZA

A New-York Style white pizza with drizzled olive oil, mozzarella,

parmesan, and ricotta cheese with Italian herbs.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb. Ready-to-Bake

    Refrigerated Pizza Dough

  • 3 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • ½ teaspoon Garlic Powder

  • 16 ounces Mozzarella

    Cheese (freshly grated)

  • ½ cup Parmesan Cheese

    (freshly grated)

  • ½ teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Red Pepper Flakes

STEPS

  1. Start with ready-to-bake refrigerated pizza dough. Toss the

    dough and using fingers, spread onto pizza pan. Generously

    drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil. Sprinkle with garlic powder.

  2. Top with freshly grated mozzarella. I prefer to use a block of

    mozzarella cheese and freshly grate it before sprinkling on the

    pizza. It melts so much better than shredded cheese.

  3. In a small bowl, stir together ricotta cheese, Parmesan

    Cheese, and salt. This is what gives the ricotta cheese extra

    flavor so it isn't bland!

  4. Generously sprinkle with Italian herbs.

  5. Bake at high heat-- 450 degrees to allow the crust to crisp

    on the bottom.
